Our Team

Our highly-trained office professionals place an old-fashioned emphasis on personalized customer service. We work hard to see that our clients' needs and accounts are serviced efficiently and confidentially keeping in mind that our clients are the most important aspect of our business.

Thomas A. Nestlehut
Financial Advisor

Tom has been offering his services to his clients for over 40 years. He firmly believes that clients want to discuss their planning needs in person. By discussing their desires and listening to their concerns, he can establish a relationship that can last a lifetime. "We are not transactionally focused, we are relationship people. By creating a high level of trust and confidence, we will help you with your financial objectives."

Tom personally enjoys addressing financial planning objectives and issues with his clients. He has also been invited to be a media contributor when discussing financial planning issues. He has authored numerous planning articles in various newspapers and co-authored a book titled, "Wealth Enhancement and Preservation."
