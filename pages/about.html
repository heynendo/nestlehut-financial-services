Since 1981, Nestlehut Financial has provided comprehensive insurance products to their clients. In 1988, additional services were added specializing in retirement planning, IRA rollovers, and investments which include non-retirement products. We have had the privilege of helping hundreds of our clients manage their assets and transition into retirement. Yet, our services are not unusual. In fact, many other financial planners offer some of the same assistance. What makes us unique is the quality of the service we provide.

We believe we have the best client service in the industry and strive daily to make it even better. We will sit down and discuss your finances, concerns, current needs and future goals. Together, we will work to arrive at a method for pursuing those goals. New clients are often pleasantly surprised at the comfort level they experience in their first meeting, and we will make sure that experience continues as our relationship grows.

2024 Five Star Wealth Manager Award Winner

The Five Star Wealth Manager award, administered by Crescendo Business Services, LLC (dba Five Star Professional), is based on 10 objective criteria.

  1. 1. Credentialed as a registered investment adviser or a registered investment adviser representative;
  2. 2. Actively registered as a registered investment adviser or as a principal of a registered investment adviser firm for a minimum of 5 years;
  3. 3. Favorable regulatory and complaint history review (As defined by Five Star Professional, the wealth manager has not:
    1. A. Been subject to a regulatory action that resulted in a registration being suspended or revoked, or payment of a fine;
    2. B. Had more than a total of three settled or pending complaints filed against them and/or a total of five settled, pending, dismissed or denied complaints with any regulatory authority or Five Star Professional's consumer complaint process. Unfavorable feedback may have been discovered through a check of complaints registered with a regulatory authority or complaints registered through Five Star Professional's consumer complaint process; feedback may not be representative of any one client's experience;
    3. C. Individually contributed to a financial settlement of a customer complaint;
    4. D. Filed for personal bankruptcy within the past 11 years;
    5. E. Been terminated from a financial services firm within the past 11 years;
    6. F. Been convicted of a felony);
  4. 4. Fulfilled their firm review based on internal standards;
  5. 5. Accepting new clients. Evaluation criteria - considered:
  6. 6. One-year client retention rate;
  7. 7. Five-year client retention rate;
  8. 8. Non-institutional discretionary and/or non-discretionary client assets administered;
  9. 9. Number of client households served;
  10. 10. Education and professional designations.
